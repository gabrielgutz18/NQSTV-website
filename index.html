About Us

Welcome to Inquisitive

Your trusted partner in contract and cost consultancy services. We are committed to delivering innovative solutions that meet your needs and exceed your expectations. We also offer a special Design to Cost services for our clients to optimize the project budget.

NQSTV COST AND CONTRACT TRAINING AND CONSULTANCY SERVICES “iNQuiSiTiVe” is a sole-proprietor training and consulting service provider which aims to help the stakeholders of the Philippine Construction Industry understand the International standards used in Quantity Surveying, particularly Cost and Contract.

Our Services

Structured support before and after contract award.

INQuiSiTiVe offers an alternative option for construction professionals who spend busy days in the office and at the site. With the Cost and Contract corporate trainings, the employees have the benefit of learning during office hours, with the comfort of their office or homes and without sacrificing their weekends.
