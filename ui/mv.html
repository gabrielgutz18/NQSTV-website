Mission and Vision

Guided by better standards, broader access, and dependable quantity surveying practice.

Our direction is not limited to service delivery. We aim to strengthen the Cost and Contract Management and Quantity Surveying industry through advocacy, education, and practical client support.

Our Mission

What we are committed to doing consistently.

In order to achieve our vision, we are dedicated to:

  • Providing and maintaining the best quality QS services to clients.
  • Promoting Cost and Contract Management as an integral part of Project Management.
  • Making education accessible to both Metro Manila and provincial areas.
  • Offering affordable training without compromising quality.

How We Move

Three working priorities behind the mission.

01

Deliver strong service quality

Client trust starts with reliable QS outputs, careful documentation, and commercially sound support.

02

Expand professional understanding

We continue advocating for cost and contract management as a core component of responsible project management.

03

Keep knowledge accessible

Education should remain practical and reachable for learners across both urban and provincial settings.

Core Values

Principles that shape how we teach, advise, and deliver.

These values are the operating standard behind our client relationships, our training philosophy, and our contribution to the industry.

Advocacy

We accept that educating Filipino constructors is not an easy task, and we accept the challenge with seriousness and purpose.

Continuity

We promote the idea that education is a never-ending process and that professional growth should remain active throughout a career.

Integrity

We recognize that honesty and transparency are essential qualities in the conduct of a quantity surveyor and in the trust clients place in us.

Value for Money

We make sure each client receives work that is worthwhile, dependable, and aligned with the level of trust they place in our service.
