Our Vision

To become one of the leading

advocates in promulgating the Cost and Contract Management

and Quantity Surveying Industry in the Philippines.

Mission

In order to achieve our vision, we are dedicated to:

• Providing and maintaining the best quality QS Services to clients

• Promoting Cost and Contract Management as an integral part of Project Management

• Making education accessible to both Metro Manila and provincial areas

• Offering affordable training without compromising quality

Our Core Values

Advocacy

We accept that educating Filipino Constructors is not an easy task but we accept the challenge.

Continuity

We promote that education is a never-ending process.

Integrity

We recognize that honesty and transparency is an essential conduct of a quantity surveyor.

Value for Money

We make sure that each client gets what if not more than what they paid for.
